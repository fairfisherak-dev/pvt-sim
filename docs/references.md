## Reading Priority

### Tier 1: Must Read Before Implementation
1. Peng-Robinson (1976) — 6 pages
2. Rachford-Rice (1952) — 2 pages
3. Michelsen (1982a, 1982b) — Stability and flash
4. Pedersen (1984) — Plus-fraction splitting

### Tier 2: Read When Implementing Module
5. Riazi-Daubert (1987) — When implementing correlations
6. Lohrenz-Bray-Clark (1964) — When implementing viscosity
7. Weinaug-Katz (1943) — When implementing IFT
8. Nojabaei et al. (2013) — When implementing confinement

### Tier 3: Reference as Needed
- Textbooks (Pedersen et al., Whitson & Brulé)
- Historical papers (van der Waals, Redlich-Kwong)
- Alternative correlations (Kesler-Lee, Cavett)
